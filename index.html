Welcome to Acelin German Shepherds.

We are small kennel located in the town of Yelm, Washington. Our dogs spend most of the time in the freedom of our back yard. We bring the best lines from Europe, usually daughters and sons of VA1 like Ober von Bad Boll or 2X VA1 Vegas du Haut Mansard. Our female from Ober just won first place in her first show and is a very promising dog. Our stud who is the son of Vegas just got SG2 and he is very promising and is being titled at this time. We are a new breeder in this area but we bring 48 years of experience from Europe in German Shepherd breeding. All of our dogs are show and breeding quality as well as wonderful companions for any family.
