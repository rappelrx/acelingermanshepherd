About Us

We are located in beautiful Yelm, Washington. At Acelin German Shepherds we strive to provide the best temperament with world class bloodlines to produce the best protection and family companion. Our goal is to maintain the German Shepherd excellent breed by bringing together the highest quality of show and working lines directly from Europe.  All of our German Shepherd puppies come with health guarantee.

This magnificent dog its known for its beauty, loyalty, athletic ability and its desire and love to work. The German Shepherd excels at the sport of Schutzhund and is also well known for military and police work.

More importantly German Shepherd is recognized for its loyalty and dedication to their family. They are natural protectors, love children and are faithful companion.

Come visit us and pick up a puppy that is right for you. Our puppies come with health guarantee and AKC registration papers. We can also ship your puppy to you anywhere in the world depending on your country requirements.
